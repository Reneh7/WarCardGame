Rules

War is a simple card game typically played by two players. The deck is divided evenly between the players, and each player places their stack of cards face down. During each round, both players reveal the top card of their stack simultaneously. The player with the higher-ranked card wins the round and collects both cards, placing them to the side. If the cards are of equal rank, a "war" occurs: each player places two face-down cards on top of their revealed card and then reveals a third card. The player with the higher-ranked third card wins all the cards in the "war." The game continues until they go through all of their cards and the winner is the player who captured more cards.
